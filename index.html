What is pâtisserie?

Pâtisserie is used to describe French pastries

and the pastry shop they are sold in. Although

the word is used quite liberally in English-speaking

countries, in France and Belgium the law restricts

its use to bakeries who employ licensed

maître pâtissier (master pastry chefs).
